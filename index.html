1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

IT in modern business is needed to communicate fast and efficiently with individuals, organisations or IT systems. Modern businesses also rely on IT to process data and keep up with market intelligence. IT in modern business plays an important role in all types of industry, which helps improve processes within a business, help to competitively maintain an advantage in the marketplace and achieve cost efficiencies.

1.2 Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully – what do you need to make projects successful? What tools? What skills?

Depending on the project and whether or not you are working individually or collaboratively within a team, there are methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully. Methods I would use are documenting all research and sharing it within my team if it is a collaboration. I would also implement regular reviews (stand-ups) to monitor progress. Tools which could be useful are but not limited to; Trello, Slack and Google Drive. Trello can be used as a digital version of a kanban board to monitor progress of tasks within a project (what needs to be done, what is being worked on and what has been completed). Slack is a great tool for communicating with a team working on a project from anywhere in the world. Features of Slack; video calling, sharing code, text, images to keep up to date with all aspects of a project. Google Drive can be used to create, edit, store and share different types of documents with individuals which helps to keep content fresh. Skills needed would have to be a good knowledge of aspects of a project with a good ability to problem solve.
